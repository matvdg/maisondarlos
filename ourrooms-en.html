    Our guest rooms


    Our two rooms are inspired by the Pyrenees around us, with a view of the mountain where colors come in with the seasons.
    All furniture has been designed by us in solid elm, local and durable wood. The bedding is new and of quality.

    A rich breakfast is included in the room price.

    Both bedrooms have access to a shared bathroom and toilet. Cleaning is done daily with environmentally friendly homemade products.

    Room "Aneto"


    The Aneto room takes its name from the highest peak in the Pyrenees culminating at 3404m, not far from our valley.

    80€/night with breakfast included.
    Sheets and towels provided.
    Welcome drink offered.

    Room "Pic du Gar"

    Room "Pic du Gar"


    The Pic du Gar is the peak closest to us, culminating at 1785m above sea level, visible from our garden. Enough to inspire you for your next hikes!

    80€/night with breakfast included.
    Sheets and towels provided.
    Welcome drink offered.

    Breakfast

    Breakfast


    Our breakfast is served at your request between 8am and 10am. It is greedy and local. We make our own homemade bread with organic flour from our region and we offer homemade jams and honey from the valley! In addition to the traditional breakfast, we concoct a little sweet or savoury treat according to your tastes every morning! On option, we can also organize a brunch.

    Common areas

    Common areas


    Our lounge is open to you for your relaxation. You can have a drink, play board games or read a good book in the heat of the stove. A library is available upstairs, we have a few books in English but it's time to learn French ;-) In summer, you will eat on our terrace to enjoy the garden and the mountain view.
